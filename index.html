Lion

A lion's presence in the African bush is felt more than the animal is actually seen. Signs are all around. Hand-sized tracks in the soft earth after a rain; a bloody carcass, jutting with bones and left for scavengers; a distant "harumping" roar. To actually witness this beast-king is to sense death on four legs.

Leopard

The leopard is the master of stealth and is the most elusive of The Big 5. With a coat that blends with its shadowy surroundings, soundless movements and a knack for asassination, the leopard is the most successful big cat in the world.
